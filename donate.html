Do you know what is Pareto Principle?

Pareto Principle

Rudimentary explanation: 80% of consequences come from 20% of causes
For example:

   • Raising Funds: 20% of the donors contribute 80% of the total
   • Life: 20% of your works dedicate to 80% of your success
   • Social: 20% richest people of the world receiving 82.7% of the world's income
